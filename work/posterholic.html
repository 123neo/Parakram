“'A man is known by the design he keeps.'” - Anonymous

Parakram'18 brings to you Posterholic, a challenge designed to test your creativity and photoshop skills.

Problem Statement :

  • Design a poster that have a clear induction with, this years Parakram's theme :'Uttopia of Illusion'

Rounds and details :

  • It is an online facebook event.
  • The event is open from 5th April to 14th April(midnight)
  • Participants are to make an A4 size poster (high resolution) inculcating the theme of the fest.
  • The poster(.psd file) has to be send before 13th April to the Parakram's official Facebook page : www.fb.com/ietparakram

Judging Criteria :

  • The posters that will satisfy a particular benchmark for uniquenes and originality will be posted on facebook.
  • Each REACT (with also, the person LIKING THE PAGE) will fetch 1 point. A SHARE to the post will fetch 3 points.

Rules and regulation :

  • Individual participation
  • The participants must make sure that their design is not someone's else work. Usage of online resources is permitted, but plagiarism won't to be tolerated.
  • Paricipants using explicit designing methods and enhanced creativity will get additional 50 points from the judges.
